About NaCo

Junior Silubane better known as NaCo is a Producer /Disc Jockey born in the East Rand of Johannesburg, Tsakane. My love for music goes way back as this journey began when I was 13, taught myself how to DJ & 2 years later, jumped right into production & I've never looked back since then. I live music. I breathe music & very passionate about what I do. I'm versatile that I'd describe my sound as a mixture of a lot sounds, pre- dominantly Afro-Tech as the likes of DJ SHIMZA/Black Coffee have been a huge inspiration to me.
